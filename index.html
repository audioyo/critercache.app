The Features

Everything your quest needs

🗺️

Adventure-Themed Tasks

Morning routines, evening chores, weekly quests — organized into groups that earn coins on completion. Bonus timers turn getting dressed into a speed challenge.

→ 3 routine types
🫙

The Reward Jar

Coins fill a glass jar, like a kid's homemade piggy bank, toward a parent-defined goal. When it overflows, confetti and a critter dance follow. Kids see exactly how close they are, every single day.

→ Visual motivation
🔑

Parent-Powered Control

PIN-gated admin handles everything: task creation, reward management, batch approvals, and bonus coin awards. Real-time or end-of-day — your schedule, your rules.

→ PIN-protected
⚡

Speed Bonus Timers

Optional countdown timers make tasks into mini-challenges. Beat the clock for a bonus coin. Miss it? No penalty — the task still counts. Excitement, zero stress.

→ Bonus mode only
📅

Streak Tracking

A full monthly map-calendar shows completion history with color-coded days. Streaks are celebrated. One grace day per week keeps momentum on rough days.

→ Non-punitive design
✨

Surprise Find Bonuses

Award spontaneous bonus coins from the parent panel. The critter "finds" a treasure item — a golden feather, a shiny acorn — making good behavior feel magical and rare.

→ Rare collectibles
The Method

How the Quest Works

🐾

Pick Your Critter

Each child chooses their companion and gives them a name. That critter lives on their home screen and reacts to every completed task.

☑️

Complete Quests

Morning routines, evening tasks, extras — check them off and watch coins fly into the bank. Parents confirm in real-time or batch-approve at bedtime.

🫙

Fill the Jar

Coins fill toward a chosen reward goal. The jar shows progress at a glance. When full, the confetti cannon fires and the critter dances.

🎁

Claim the Reward

Kids redeem their goal with parent confirmation. The reward joins a permanent Trophy Case — a history of every adventure completed.
